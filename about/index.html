About Me

I am a currently in the process of graduating from Sheffield Hallam University having finished my studies towards a Masters in Games Design. I have been a part of a number of group projects whilst at university as both a team member and also lead artist - most notably working as a lead artist in porting Piecefall from PS4 to mobile with extended game play.

As a 3D artist I enjoy creating artwork in a variety of styles from realistic to handpainted - with a particular interest in fantasy themes which has fuelled a few of my personal projects.

Currently I am London based and available to work as of June 2016.

I can be contacted at: johnscribbins@gmail.com
